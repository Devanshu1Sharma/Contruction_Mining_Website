Business Activities
  • 01/QEM Group

    Construction aggregates are the most mined building material in the world and for good reason — the right stone aggregates add resistance and strength to the concrete or mortar.

  • 02/Mining Estate

    A mining estate refers to a designated area of land that has been set aside for the extraction of minerals, ores, or other valuable geological materials. This land is often owned or leased by mining companies and is specifically utilized for mining operations.

  • 03/Mineral Processing

    Mineral processing, also known as ore dressing, is the process of separating commercially valuable minerals from their ores. This process involves several stages, including crushing, grinding, and beneficiation, to produce a final product that is suitable for further use or sale..

  • 04/Logistics

    Logistics refers to the efficient management of the flow of goods, services, and information from the point of origin to the point of consumption. For a stone crushing company, logistics plays a critical role in ensuring that raw materials are transported to the processing site and that finished products are delivered to customers in a timely and cost-effective manner.

  • 05/Real Estate

    Real estate refers to land and any permanent structures on it, such as buildings, homes, and other property. It encompasses a broad range of activities, including the buying, selling, renting, and developing of properties. The real estate industry is vital to the economy, influencing various aspects of individual and business finances.
